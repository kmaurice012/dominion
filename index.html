About Hev Tro Silage Presen U wickeln
Farm and company

Our family have farmed here since 1800. Dominion Farms is the lifetime achievement of our grandad, Hintzen. His grandchildren – Maurice, Junior, Grace and Paul – are now working hard to build a modern, sustainable countryside business on the foundations he laid. Maurice graduated in Computer Science and worked in London before returning to Dominion Farms to take on the farm full-time in 2020. The bell tents proved a popular addition to the site last season in the wake of the Covid-19 Pandemic, and this season we have added more on-site activities and facilities for glampers. We have re-opened the fishing lake to the public, and the plant nursery is once more growing organic salad and veg. We hope you you enjoy browsing our website, and hope you will pay us a visit soon! Please feel free to drop us a message or give us a call, anytime.

Dominion Farms has helped us recruit and retain great intern applicants. Paul Jobs
Partner

I can not say enough about what a positive and valuable experience working with the Dominions Farms, and having a our own fabulous intern has been for the farm and me personally. Every part of the program, from the beginning to end was well thought out and executed. We are very much looking forward to next year and being part of this again. I would highly recommend participating in this program to any farm that has space for an intern. Dominion Farms has taken the time to plug in with some of the best family scale farms and farmers in Germany…this is an awesome opportunity to ‘break’ into agriculture and arrive in a supportive community.
